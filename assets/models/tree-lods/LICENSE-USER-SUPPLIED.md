# User-supplied tree LOD pack

The project owner supplied `tree_lods.zip` for inclusion in Ashenhold. The archive contained `tree_LOD0.glb`, `tree_LOD1.glb`, and `tree_LOD2.glb`, but no author, source URL, copyright notice, or license metadata.

The runtime files in this directory are deployment-oriented derivatives of that supplied archive. Mesh and animation buffer data are retained at the three supplied levels of detail. Repeated embedded textures were deduplicated, the two color textures were transcoded to WebP, and unsupported/unused normal and packed material maps were omitted from the browser build.

These files are included on the project owner's representation that they are authorized for use in this project. They are not offered as a standalone asset pack, and this notice does not grant rights beyond the authorization applicable to Ashenhold.
